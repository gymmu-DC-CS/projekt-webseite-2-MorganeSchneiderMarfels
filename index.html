Introduction

Romantic comedies, or rom-coms, are movies that combine love stories with funny and heartwarming situations. They focus on characters finding romance, often with awkward moments, misunderstandings, or hilarious challenges along the way. Rom-coms usually follow a simple pattern: two people meet, they face obstacles (sometimes comedic, sometimes emotional), and by the end, they fall in love or learn something important about relationships. These films are popular because they make us laugh, feel emotional, and root for the couple.
